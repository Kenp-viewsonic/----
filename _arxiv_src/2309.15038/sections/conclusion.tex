\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}
In this paper, we develop a more holistic OCL method named HPCR based on PCR, which mainly consists of three components. The contrastive component conditionally introduces anchor-to-sample pairs to PCR, improving the feature extraction ability of PCR when the training batch size is large. The temperature component decouples the influence of temperature on gradients in two parts and effectively improves the generalization ability of the model. The distillation component improves the anti-forgetting ability of the model through PCD and SCD. Extensive experiments on four datasets demonstrate the superiority of HPCR over various state-of-the-art methods.
